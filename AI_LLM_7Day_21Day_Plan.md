## **📝 QUESTION BREAKDOWN (21 Days)**

**Per Day:** 15 Detailed Questions + 20 MCQs = 35 items
**Total: 21 × 35 = 735 items**

### **Question Distribution by Type:**

**Detailed Questions (15/day):**
1. Conceptual Understanding (3-4 questions)
   - "Explain why attention is better than RNNs"
   - "What is the key difference between BERT and GPT?"

2. Mathematical/Technical (4-5 questions)
   - "Calculate the output of softmax attention"
   - "Walk through a backpropagation example"

3. Real-world Application (3-4 questions)
   - "How would you use RAG for a customer support chatbot?"
   - "Design a fine-tuning strategy for domain-specific LLM"

4. Comparison & Analysis (2-3 questions)
   - "Compare LSTM vs Transformer for sequence modeling"
   - "When should you use LoRA vs full fine-tuning?"

**MCQs (20/day):**
- 5 Easy (definition, recall)
- 8 Medium (application, comparison)
- 5 Hard (synthesis, edge cases)
- 2 Research/Future (cutting edge topics)
